Peanut Butter Protein Shake

Home

Ingredients

  • frozen banana
  • vanilla protein powder
  • milk
  • peanut butter
  • ice

Steps

  1. Slice 1 frozen banana and put into a blender
  2. add 2 tablespoons of your favorite peanut butter
  3. add 2 scoops of any vanilla protein powder
  4. add 1 cup of ice cubes
  5. add 1/4 cup of milk
  6. blend to a consistency of your liking

Enjoy!
